# PIMS Scope

## Scope statement

Social Juridico operates a Privacy Information Management System to govern the processing of personal data in the platform, including data from clients, lawyers, office members, administrators and public website users.

The PIMS extends the ISO/IEC 27001 ISMS scope and covers privacy controls for collection, use, storage, sharing, deletion, retention and audit evidence.

## In scope

- Account registration and login.
- Lawyer and client profiles.
- Office enterprise staff and intern accounts.
- Case, message and document workflows when they contain personal data.
- LGPD deletion and purge workflows.
- Authentication and security audit logs.
- Vendor processing through Supabase, Resend, Stripe, hosting and analytics providers.

## Out of scope

- Personal devices not managed by Social Juridico.
- External systems operated directly by lawyers, clients or offices outside the platform.
- Third-party websites reached through outbound links after the user leaves Social Juridico.

## Privacy objectives

- Process personal data lawfully and transparently.
- Minimize personal data exposed through APIs and reports.
- Maintain traceability of sensitive privacy operations.
- Support data subject rights under LGPD.
- Retain logs and evidence without storing unnecessary raw personal data.
