# Privacy Incident Procedure

## Definition

A privacy incident is any confirmed or suspected unauthorized access, disclosure, loss, alteration or improper processing of personal data.

## Triage

Assess:

- Data subjects affected.
- Categories of personal data involved.
- Whether sensitive data is involved.
- Whether data was encrypted, hashed or otherwise protected.
- Whether the event affects controller or processor obligations.
- Required notification timeline under applicable law or contract.

## Response

1. Contain the event.
2. Preserve logs and evidence.
3. Assess privacy impact.
4. Notify internal security/privacy owners.
5. Decide whether external notification is required.
6. Record corrective actions.
7. Update risk register and controls.

## Evidence

Keep:

- Incident timeline.
- Logs and audit events.
- Scope analysis.
- Notification decision.
- Corrective action record.
