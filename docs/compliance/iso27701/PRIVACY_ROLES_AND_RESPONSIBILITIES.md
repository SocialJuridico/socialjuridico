# Privacy Roles and Responsibilities

## Social Juridico as controller

Social Juridico acts as controller for platform account data, authentication data, billing operations, product telemetry, support records and security audit evidence.

Responsibilities:

- Define purposes and lawful basis for processing.
- Maintain privacy notices and internal procedures.
- Respond to data subject requests.
- Select and review processors.
- Maintain privacy incident response process.

## Social Juridico as processor

Social Juridico may act as processor where lawyers, offices or enterprise customers enter client case data and determine the purpose of legal service processing.

Responsibilities:

- Process data according to platform terms and customer instructions.
- Protect customer-controlled personal data.
- Support deletion, export or restriction workflows where technically feasible.
- Notify relevant parties of privacy incidents according to contract and law.

## Key operational roles

| Role | Responsibility |
|---|---|
| Security owner | Owns ISMS/PIMS controls and evidence. |
| Privacy owner | Owns PII inventory, LGPD requests and privacy notices. |
| Engineering owner | Implements technical controls and audit logging. |
| Administrator | Executes operational deletion and review workflows. |
| Vendor owner | Reviews processors and privacy clauses. |
